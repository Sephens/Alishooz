About Us

We offer a large variety of name brand shoes with options to buy, sell, trade. While some of the authentic shoes offered on our site are brand new, we do also offer resell shoes, ones that we got from loyal customers just like yourself. Not only that, but we also offer trades, depending on the shoe and quality offered. This way, if you opt to clean out some of your shoe collection, you can get a different pair to easily replace it with. Our buy, sell, trade is a great option for a variety of reasons.
